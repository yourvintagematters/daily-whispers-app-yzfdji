## Stripe Configuration

### Important: Stripe Account Settings

To accept AUD payments, ensure your Stripe account is configured correctly:

1. **Log in to Stripe Dashboard**: https://dashboard.stripe.com

2. **Enable AUD Currency**:
   - Go to Settings > Payment methods
   - Ensure AUD is enabled as a supported currency
   - If not enabled, click "Add currency" and select AUD

3. **Bank Account**:
   - For receiving AUD payments, you should have an Australian bank account connected
   - Go to Settings > Bank accounts and public details
   - Add an Australian bank account if not already added

4. **Pricing**:
   - Stripe fees for AUD transactions: 1.75% + $0.30 AUD per successful card charge
   - International cards may have additional fees

## Payment Processing

### How It Works

1. **Customer enters payment details**
2. **Amount is sent to Stripe in cents**: 
   - AUD $9.99 → 999 cents
3. **Stripe processes in AUD**
4. **Payment confirmation returned**
5. **Receipt shows AUD amount**

### Currency Conversion

- **No conversion needed**: Customers with Australian cards pay directly in AUD
- **International cards**: Stripe handles conversion automatically
  - Customer's bank may charge foreign transaction fees
  - Exchange rate determined by customer's card issuer

## Tax Considerations

### GST (Goods and Services Tax)

**Important**: In Australia, digital products may be subject to 10% GST.

**Current Implementation**: Prices shown are inclusive of any applicable taxes.

**Recommendations**:
1. **Consult a tax professional** to determine if you need to charge GST
2. If GST applies, you have two options:
   - **Include GST in price**: AUD $9.99 (includes $0.91 GST)
   - **Add GST at checkout**: AUD $9.99 + $1.00 GST = AUD $10.99

3. **Update Stripe settings** to handle tax collection if required
