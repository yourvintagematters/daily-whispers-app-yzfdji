## Full Description

### iOS App Store (4000 characters max)

**Give the Gift of Daily Inspiration**

Daily Whispers is a unique gift-based app that lets you send 365 days of inspiration, love, and positivity to the people who matter most. Choose from beautifully curated quote collections, and your recipient will receive a new uplifting message every single day for an entire year.

**8 Beautiful Quote Collections**

🤍 **You Are Loved** - Celebrating self-worth and reminding us we are appreciated
😂 **The Funny Side of Life** - 365 days of laughter and humor about being human
💎 **Gratitude Pearls** - Daily reminders to appreciate life's simple joys
🍃 **Whispers from Nature** - Inspiration from the natural world
👶 **Motherhood** - Celebrating the journey of motherhood with love and wisdom
🐱 **A Cat's Life** - Feline philosophy celebrating independence and self-love
🐕 **A Dog's Life** - Canine wisdom about loyalty, love, and living in the moment
🤝 **Threads of Connection** - Affirming human connection and community

**Perfect for Every Occasion**
- Birthdays
- Anniversaries
- Thank you gifts
- Long-distance relationships
- Self-care and personal growth
- Supporting a friend through tough times
- Just because!

**Features**
✨ 365 unique quotes per collection
📱 Daily push notifications
💝 Easy gift sending
📤 Share quotes with friends
💾 Save favorite quotes to your photo library
🎨 Beautiful, themed designs
🎁 Bundle options available

**Pricing**
- Individual Collections: AUD $9.99
- Besties Bundle (3 collections): AUD $18.99
- Share the Love Big Time (10 collections): AUD $49.99

**Not a Subscription**
Daily Whispers is a simple, one-time purchase gift that lasts a year. No recurring charges, no automatic renewals - just 365 days of daily inspiration.

**How It Works**
1. Choose a quote collection that resonates
2. Preview sample quotes
3. Purchase for yourself or as a gift
4. Enter recipient details (if gifting)
5. They receive a beautiful notification each day with a new quote

**Why Daily Whispers?**
In our busy lives, we often forget to tell the people we love how much they mean to us. Daily Whispers does it for you - every single day. It's more than just quotes; it's a daily reminder that someone cares.

**Privacy & Security**
Your privacy matters. We use secure payment processing through Stripe and never store your credit card information. See our privacy policy for details.

**Support**
Questions? Contact us at [your-support-email@example.com]

Download Daily Whispers today and start spreading joy, one quote at a time! 💫
