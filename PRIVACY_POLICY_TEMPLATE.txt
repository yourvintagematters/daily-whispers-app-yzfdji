# Privacy Policy for Daily Whispers

**Last Updated**: [Current Date]

## Introduction

Daily Whispers ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Email Address**: When you create an account or make a purchase
- **Payment Information**: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store credit card details)
- **Gift Recipient Information**: Name and email address when sending quotes as gifts
- **User Preferences**: Notification settings and theme selections

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App features used, quotes viewed, interaction patterns
- **Notification Data**: Delivery status and interaction with notifications

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the Daily Whispers service
- Process payments and send purchase confirmations
- Deliver daily quote notifications
- Send quotes to gift recipients
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Respond to customer support requests
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

- **Data Storage**: Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a secure cloud database service
- **Payment Processing**: All payment information is processed through Stripe, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor
- **Encryption**: Data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS protocols
- **Access Control**: We implement strict access controls to protect your data

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### Stripe (Payment Processing)
- Purpose: Process payments securely
- Data Shared: Payment information, email address
- Privacy Policy: https://stripe.com/privacy

### Supabase (Database & Backend)
- Purpose: Store user data and app content
- Data Shared: User account information, preferences, purchase history
- Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

### Expo (App Development Platform)
- Purpose: App development and distribution
- Data Shared: Device information, crash reports
- Privacy Policy: https://expo.dev/privacy

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **With Gift Recipients**: When you send a quote gift, we share your name and the quote content
- **Service Providers**: With trusted third-party services that help us operate the app
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights
- **Business Transfers**: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data
- **Opt-Out**: Disable notifications at any time
- **Data Portability**: Request your data in a portable format

## Children's Privacy

Daily Whispers is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## Notifications

- **Daily Quotes**: We send daily notifications with your selected quotes
- **Purchase Confirmations**: We send notifications about successful purchases
- **Gift Notifications**: Recipients receive notifications about gifted quotes
- **Control**: You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: We retain your data while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion
